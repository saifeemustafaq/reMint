# **1. Bank Account Aggregation**

You will be able to view **all your financial accounts** in one place using Plaid/Yodlee-like aggregators, including:

* Bank accounts
* Credit cards
* Loans
* Investments
* 401(k), IRA, Roth
* Bills
* Property (via Zillow)

The system will pull:

* Balances
* Transactions
* Due dates
* Credit utilization

---

# **2. Auto-Categorizing Transactions**

Each transaction will be read and automatically assigned to categories such as:

* Groceries
* Restaurants
* Transportation
* Rent
* Shopping
* Utilities
* Entertainment
* Travel

You will also be able to:

* Add **custom categories**
* Split a single transaction into multiple categories
* Recategorize anything manually (the system will learn your patterns)

---

# **3. Budgeting (With Real-Time Tracking)**

You will be able to create **monthly category budgets**, for example:

* Groceries $400
* Eating Out $150
* Gas $120
* Shopping $100
* Utilities $80

The system will automatically:

* Track spending in real time
* Show progress bars
* Warn you when you are close to or over budget
* Show historical month-to-month spending

This will be extremely powerful for understanding your habits.

---

# **4. Net Worth Tracking**

The system will calculate your real net worth across all accounts:

**Assets – Liabilities = Net Worth**

It will update automatically every day, providing a simple “wealth dashboard.”

---

# **5. Bills & Reminders**

The system will scan your transactions and login accounts to detect:

* Credit card due dates
* Upcoming bills
* Subscriptions
* Annual payments

Then it will send:

* Alerts
* Emails
* Push notifications

It will effectively act as a **bill reminder system**.

---

# **6. Spending Insights, Trends & Reports**

The platform will provide analytics such as:

* Top spending categories
* Biggest merchants
* Trends over the last 3/6/12 months
* Income vs. expenses
* Spending vs. budget
* Average monthly cost per category
* Cash flow charts

This will make it easy to see:

* *Where your money goes*
* *How it changes over time*

---

# **7. Goal Planning**

The system will support savings goals, including:

* Emergency fund
* Down payment
* Paying off debt
* Vacation savings
* Large purchase planning

You will be able to assign accounts to goals and track progress.

---

# **8. Alerts**

You will be alerted for:

* Large transactions
* Bank fees
* Low balances
* Unusual spending
* Budget exceeded
* Upcoming bills

It will act as a financial safeguard.

---

# **9. Free Credit Score**

The system will offer a free credit score (not FICO, but VantageScore 3.0) with:

* Credit factors
* Score history
* Credit usage
* Missed payments

This feature will be integrated automatically.

---

# **10. Investment Tracking**

You will be able to import your investment accounts and view:

* Holdings
* Balances
* Gains/losses
* Allocation

It will not be super advanced, but will be sufficient for a casual user.

---

# **11. Subscription Tracking**

The platform will identify recurring charges and highlight:

* Streaming services
* Gyms
* Utilities
* Subscriptions you might want to cancel

This will make it easy to clean up recurring costs.
